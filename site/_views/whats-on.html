Porpoise

The seas around Wester Ross are home to a multitude of marine mammals – seals, porpoises, dolphins, otters and whales. www.porpoise-gairloch.co.uk plus www.seawildlife.co.uk

School Holidays

Held the first weekend of the summer school holidays in July http://gairlochgathering.com/

Pony Trek

A pony trekking centre is situated within walking distance further up the glen. For more info www.gairlochtrekkingcentre.co.uk

Landscape

Landscapes of Wester Ross have it all – towering mountains, glorious sweeping sandy beaches, miles of breathtaking unspoilt coastline, sea and hill lochs, rich forests and wild open moorland. Here you can find freedom, space and solitude in abundance.

Walks

There are many fine walks and climbs locally, and for the hardened mountain trekker a range of challenges are within easy reach with over 25 munros ( over 3,000ft) only a short drive away such as An Teallach in the Fisher field and Slioch, Liathach and Bienn Eighe in Torridon.

Gardens

The world famous Inverewe Gardens are only 7 miles away. The Perfume Studio, Scotland’s only working perfume studio is just a short-drive north. A unique experience not to be missed and a great place to pick up that special present. An ‘illicit still’ is another unique place to visit, again, just a short-drive north to Aultbea. See how whisky was secretly made over 200 years ago, hidden from the keen eyes of the customs and excise officers.

Beaches

There is so much to do; safe swimming from many golden beaches; superb bird watching; sailing & marine activities can be enjoyed from Gairloch Harbour; visit the award winning local heritage museum; or you can participate in a variety of activities at Gairloch leisure center.

Golf

If you want more from your holiday than soaking up the magnificent scenery, Wester Ross has plenty of other attractions and activities to keep holidaymakers fully occupied.
Gairloch Golf Club – a challenging and scenic 9-hole ‘Highland Gem’ course set on the shore of Loch Gairloch offers glorious views to The Isle of Skye, and is only a short walk from The Old Inn www.gairlochgolfclub.com We offer our guests preferential green fees.
