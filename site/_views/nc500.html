Road

By Road
From Inverness take the A9 north to the Tore roundabout, exit on the A835 direction Ullapool. Just past the village of Garve turn left on to the A832 to Achnasheen and continue on the A832 through the mountains and glens for the next 45 miles all the way to Gairloch .

Air

By Air
Flights to Inverness available from London Gatwick, Luton, Heathrow and Manchester, Birmingham & Norwich, Glasgow & Edinburgh then car hire from the airport.

Airport

The airport is situated on the B9039, off the A96, 10 miles to the East/North east of the city. Taxi’s and car hire are available, the number 11 bus operates during the daytime to the centre of Inverness – times available from the airport.
Inverness Airport Tel: 01667 464000

Rail

By Rail
Memorable rail trip from Inverness to Achnasheen ( on way toKyle of Lochalsh) with local bus connection to Gairloch
(check bus timetables beforehand)
All national enquiries 0845 748 4950
Connections from most major cities to Inverness then car hire from the railway station.
