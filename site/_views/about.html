Welcome to Gairloch Lodge

Gairloch History. History is all around you, from the Bronze Age hut circles by the Sand River and the remains of the vitrified fort of An Dùn by Gairloch beach to the Destitution roads and the magnificent family seat of the Clan Mackenzie in the wooded Flowerdale glen. Place names speak both of Viking invaders and of the traditional Gaelic culture (Gaelic is still spoken by many local people).

About Us

Discover top quality seafood and Highland hospitality at one Scotlands classic Inns. The Old Inn Gairloch is a Traditional Highland Coaching Inn overlooking Gairloch harbour on the west coast of Scotland.
